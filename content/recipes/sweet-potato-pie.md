## Ingredients

-   1 1/2 cups boiled mashed sweet potatoes
-   1 cup milk
-   1 egg beaten well
-   1/2 cup sugar
-   1/2 cup brown sugar
-   1 tsp salt
-   spice to taste (1/4 tsp each)

## Directions

1.  Preheat oven to 450F. Bake for 30 minutes.
